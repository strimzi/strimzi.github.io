Strimzi offers a range of support options — from official documentation and community discussions to practical tools and enterprise support. Whether you're just getting started or troubleshooting a production issue, there are resources available to help you.

Before asking a question, it's a good idea to check the documentation, quickstarts, and tutorials. If you still need help, make sure to include useful details like your Strimzi and Kubernetes versions, your environment, and any relevant logs or config snippets. You can also use the diagnostic tool to generate a ZIP file to share.
